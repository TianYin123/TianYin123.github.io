Interesting Work
======

This section is a small place for the papers, projects, and research styles that I keep returning to. I am especially drawn to work that treats reasoning as a process that should be inspectable, correctable, and grounded in real decision making.

- Process-level supervision for reasoning rather than surface-level chain-of-thought
- Trustworthy medical AI systems with interpretable or auditable intermediate steps
- Clinical workflow abstraction with agent or multi-agent design
- Multimodal reasoning work that studies failure cases instead of reporting only aggregate gains
